General Inquiries and Support:

For any questions regarding the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence for Mental Health (ICAIMH) - be it general queries, submission support, registration details, special provisions for Mexican researchers, venue and accommodation information, media and press inquiries, emergency contact during the conference, or post-conference feedback and suggestions - please reach out to us at contact@icaimh.org.

Our dedicated team is on hand to assist you with all aspects of the conference. We aim to provide timely and helpful responses to ensure your experience with ICAIMH is seamless and informative.
