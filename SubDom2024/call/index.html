CALL FOR PAPERS

Call for Papers: International Conference on Artificial Intelligence for Mental Health (ICAIMH) 2024

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence for Mental Health (ICAIMH) 2024 invites full paper submissions that explore the impactful role of artificial intelligence in mental health. This year, ICAIMH is proud to announce a unique opportunity: submissions accepted for ICAIMH 2024 will also be considered for the "Special Track on AI for Socio-Ecological Welfare" at the 32nd International Conference on Case-Based Reasoning (ICCBR 2024).

Focus Areas: We welcome full articles on topics including, but not limited to:

  • Prevention, detection, and treatment of mental health conditions.
  • Natural language processing for emotion or mental health condition recognition.
  • Intelligent agents and chatbots for mental health support.
  • Expert systems in psychology and psychiatry.
  • Computer vision and digital image processing in neuroimaging.
  • Ethical considerations in AI for mental health.
  • Human-Computer Interaction in AI-driven mental health tools.
  • IoT applications for biomarker monitoring.
  • Pattern recognition in psychological data.
  • Data science for knowledge discovery in large user-generated datasets.
  • Machine learning applications in mental health data analysis.
  • Research on mental health status at various demographic levels.
  • Serious video game development for mental health.
  • Data processing and analysis focused on emotion or mental health condition recognition.

Proceedings and Publication Benefits:Accepted papers will be included in the CEUR-WS proceedings, indexed by SCOPUS and Google Scholar. For a paper to be included, at least one author must register and present the paper live at the conference.

Submission Guidelines

  • Formatting: Follow CEUR-WS formatting instructions using the designated LaTeX or Word templates.
  • Language:Submissions must be in English.
  • Submission Platform: Submit electronically via EasyChair, indicating submission for AI for Socio-Ecological Welfare; On the option "Track", select "SPECIAL TRACK ON ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR SOCIO-ECOLOGICAL WELFARE", and on the following page, in the "Topics" section, select "Mental Health".

Important Dates:

  • Submission Deadline: April 1, 2024
  • Notification of Acceptance: April 17, 2024
  • Camera-Ready Submission: July 1, 2024

We are excited to see your contributions at ICAIMH 2024, where your research not only advances the field of AI in mental health but also stands a chance to be featured in a dedicated track at ICCBR 2024.

For any inquiries, please contact contact@icaimh.org.

Join us in bridging AI innovations with mental health solutions at ICAIMH 2024!
