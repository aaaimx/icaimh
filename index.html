REGISTRATION

The ICAIMH is pleased to announce that registration for ICAIMH-23 is now open. Please read carefully the following information provided in this section and have your credit card ready for payment

Registration cutting date: Jan 20, 2023
General Information
  1. One full registration per each accepted paper is required. The registered author has to attend and present the paper. If more than one author attends, then each author must register separately. Should COVID regulations in your country of residence prevent you from participating in-person.
  2. Full delegate registration fee includes one opening reception ticket, one banquet ticket and free tutorials.
  3. Full student registration fee includes one opening reception ticket, one student reception ticket and free tutorials
Students
  1. Students will be required to upload proof of their student status at time of registration.
  2. Student ID’s without clear dates of validity, webpage screenshots and unofficial records will NOT be accepted as proof! Validity of proof must overlap with conference dates.
