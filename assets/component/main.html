WELCOME TO ICAIMH 2023, THE INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR MENTAL HEALTH.

February 8-10, 2023
Mérida, Yucatán, México.



Welcome to the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence for Mental Health (ICAIMH) 2023! This conference aims to bring together experts from the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Mental Health to explore the latest developments, challenges, and opportunities in the application of AI in mental health. The conference will provide a platform for interdisciplinary discussions and the exchange of ideas, experiences and knowledge on the use of AI in mental health research, diagnosis, treatment and care. Join us as we delve into the exciting and rapidly evolving field of AI in mental health and its potential to improve the lives of individuals and communities worldwide. We look forward to seeing you at ICAIMH 2023.
